Egg Counter

This is a stand to easily take photos of vials always in the same position. The light system inside the stand allows you to illuminate the vial from underneath and from both sides independently.

You can control the lighting arrangement with your computer via USB.

How to use it

Manual

The stand is very easy to use, just plug into your computer the usb cable and the power supply for the lights into the mains.

You can use this image adquisition software (LINK) to connect to the stand and start taking photos.

There is three types of illumination that you can select:

  • Backlight: It creates a uniform light in the base o the vial, very useful with translucent or transparent food.
  • Right light: It illuminates the vial from the right, useful for solid food.
  • Left light: Same as the former but on the left, this helps to take two photos from both sides without moving the vial and that way the shadows can be removed by a combination of both photos.

Hardware

Hardware Files

Hardware files of the project can be found on github: Github project

The materials used in this project are acrylic sheet of 3mm in two colours, midnight black and translcent.

The electronics consist on an Arduino UNO or pro mini that drives a three separeted white cool led strips.

The stand is designed to fit a Logitech pro c920 full HD but can be redisegned to fit another webcameras.

More info on how to make it, bill of materials and explanations will be available shortly on this website.

You can buy the stand ready to use from Polygonal Tree or you can just buy it as a Kit that includes the acrylic bits cutted and the electronics.

Please contact Polygonal Tree if you need to make some changes in the design or want to order one.

Future work on this is can be to use the Raspberry pi and Pi camera to take the photos and analyze the photos for you.
